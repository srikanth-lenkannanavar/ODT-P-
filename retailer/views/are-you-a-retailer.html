are you a retailer?

Late, lost or missed deliveries cost retailers millions of pounds every year and can affect customer satisfaction and loyalty as well as damaging a reputation. Almost half of UK shoppers say they would order more online if there were better delivery options available.

Customers are also frustrated by the lack of choice when it comes to how they receive things; they have to compromise between cost, convenience and control.

But what if you could offer them all of these things, by putting them in control? It’s a simple idea that would reduce the number of missed deliveries, improve customer satisfaction, drive loyalty and give you a real competitive advantage.


What is On the dot?

It’s a revolutionary service that finally provides your customers with deliveries within a 1 hour timeslot of their choice, whether that’s today, tomorrow or next week. Your customers will love this added benefit when shopping with you.




How does it work?

There are three ways in which you can offer this service to your customers.

Integration with our open API

We can integrate into your e-commerce and EPOS systems by using our RESTful API. The integration creates a seamless experience where customers can arrange a delivery direct from your checkout page online or in-store at the till, ensuring the customer stays within your retail environment.

More info

Retailer Portal – No integration

Why not offer your customers a 1 hour delivery window without integration via our Retailer Portal? This service enables you to start offering the On the dot 1 hour deliveries to your customers as soon as your profile has been created!

onthedot.co.uk

Your customers can also arrange their deliveries independently by visiting our website, onthedot.co.uk. Shoppers buy online directly from you using your Click & Collect service, or purchase in-store or over the phone. They then simply arrange for us to pick up and deliver the items on their behalf.
