Where we collect

Not sure if we collect from your favourite shop? If your delivery postcode is in the list above and your store’s collection postcode is within 5 miles of this, On the dot can help.

No matter where you live or work, whether its W14 or E1, you can book collections from all the major shopping districts in the city. So if like Dottie, you live in E1 it means you can arrange collections from as far as Stratford, Camden, Dulwich, Crouch End, the West End or even Clapham.
